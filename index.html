Star Players to Watch

Lionel Messi

🇦🇷 Argentina  |  Forward

The 2022 World Cup winner and eight-time Ballon d'Or holder. Messi completed his football legacy by lifting the trophy in Qatar. At 38, he aims to lead Argentina in a historic title defense at WC 2026.

Kylian Mbappé

🇫🇷 France  |  Forward

France's golden boy and Real Madrid superstar. Mbappé scored a hat-trick in the 2022 final and remains the most dangerous forward on the planet. He will be France's main title hope at WC 2026.

Erling Haaland

🇳🇴 Norway  |  Striker

The Manchester City and Norway striker is one of the most prolific goal-scorers in history. A machine in front of goal, Haaland will carry Norway's WC 2026 qualification hopes on his shoulders.

Vinicius Jr

🇧🇷 Brazil  |  Forward

Real Madrid's electrifying winger and Ballon d'Or winner. Vinicius is the face of Brazil's future and aims to end the Seleção's 24-year wait for a World Cup title at the 2026 tournament.

Jude Bellingham

🇬🇧 England  |  Midfielder

The Real Madrid midfielder became a global superstar in just a few years. England's most exciting talent, Bellingham's vision, energy and goal-scoring ability make him a force at WC 2026.

Pedri

🇪🇸 Spain  |  Midfielder

Barcelona's midfield maestro is the heartbeat of the Spanish national team. With precise passing, creativity and composure beyond his years, Pedri will be central to Spain's WC 2026 campaign.

Mohamed Salah

🇪🇬 Egypt  |  Forward

Liverpool's iconic forward and Egypt's all-time leading scorer. Salah is one of the most lethal attackers in the world and will be desperate to lead Egypt to a first-ever FIFA World Cup appearance.

Harry Kane

🇬🇧 England  |  Striker

England's record goalscorer and captain. Despite missing out in previous tournaments, Kane's world-class finishing and leadership make England genuine contenders for glory at WC 2026.

Kevin De Bruyne

🇧🇪 Belgium  |  Midfielder

The Manchester City playmaker is arguably the greatest midfielder of his generation. His passing range, vision, and set-piece delivery are virtually unmatched at international level.

Lamine Yamal

🇪🇸 Spain  |  Winger

The Barcelona teenage sensation who lit up Euro 2024. Yamal's dribbling, pace and flair make him a generational talent. At just 17, he is already one of the most exciting players on Earth.

Rodri

🇪🇸 Spain  |  Midfielder

The 2024 Ballon d'Or winner and anchor of both Manchester City and Spain. Rodri's defensive intelligence and composure on the ball make him indispensable to any team he plays for.

Neymar Jr

🇧🇷 Brazil  |  Forward

Brazil's all-time leading scorer and one of the most skillful players ever. If fit, Neymar brings flair, creativity and match-winning ability that could be decisive for Brazil's WC 2026 bid.

World Cup Legends

Icons who defined the beautiful game on football's greatest stage

Pelé

🇧🇷 Brazil

The only player to win THREE World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970). Widely considered the greatest player of all time, Pelé scored 12 goals in 14 World Cup appearances.

Diego Maradona

🇦🇷 Argentina

Led Argentina to the 1986 World Cup title almost single-handedly, scoring the "Goal of the Century" and the controversial "Hand of God" goal against England in the same match.

Ronaldo (R9)

🇧🇷 Brazil

The all-time World Cup top scorer before Klose. Ronaldo scored 15 WC goals, including a brace in the 2002 final. Won the Golden Boot twice and lifted the trophy with Brazil in 2002.

Zinedine Zidane

🇫🇷 France

The magician who inspired France to WC glory in 1998 on home soil, scoring two headers in the final. Won the Golden Ball at the 2006 WC despite his infamous headbutt in the final.

Miroslav Klose

🇩🇪 Germany

The all-time leading scorer in World Cup history with 16 goals. Klose played in four World Cups (2002–2014) and won the tournament in 2014 when Germany beat Argentina 1–0 in the final.

Franz Beckenbauer

🇩🇪 Germany

One of only three men to win the World Cup as both player (1974) and manager (1990). "Der Kaiser" revolutionized the sweeper position and remains Germany's greatest ever footballer.
